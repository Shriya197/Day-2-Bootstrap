Most Powerful Avengers

Iron Man

Tony Stark is an engineering superhero! Although Tony Stark doesn't have any super powers, he is an incredibly good engineer . Iron Man is Tony Stark's creation. It's a special suit that gives Stark superhuman strength, the ability to fly and powerful weapons.

Captain America

Captain America is the alter ego of Steve Rogers, a frail young artist enhanced to the peak of human perfection by an experimental "super-soldier serum" after joining the military to aid the United States government's efforts in World War II.

Scarlet Witch

WANDA MAXIMOFF Notably powerful, Wanda Maximoff has fought both against and with the Avengers, attempting to hone her abilities and do what she believes is right to help the world. She is the most powerful avenger with enormous powers of mind control.

Captain Marvel

Captain Marvel is very powerful. She has superhuman strength, stamina, durability, and agility. Captain Marvel can also fly at great speeds, well beyond the sound barrier. Captain Marvel also has something called a “Seventh Sense” where she can sense danger. So it turns out Captain Marvel is very important, even though she's not in a huge chunk of the film

Thor

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, superhero, self-proclaimed protector of Earth and the king of Asgard. Thor made a name for himself as the mightiest warrior on his homeworld and subsequently became well known for his actions on Earth, which included acting as a founding member of the Avengers.

Doctor Strange

Strange was a skilled neurosurgeon before nerve damage impaired his hands . Doctor Strange is described as "the mightiest magician in the cosmos" by Eternity, the sentience of the Marvel Universe. Doctor Stephen Strange now serves as the Sorcerer Supreme—Earth's foremost protector against magical and mystical threats.
